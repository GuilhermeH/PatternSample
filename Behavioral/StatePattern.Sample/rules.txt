The following actions are possible for a Document: Edit, Send for review, Approve, Reject and Cancel.

Suggested rules:

Draft: Can be edited, sent for review, or canceled.
In Review: Cannot be edited, but can be approved, rejected, or canceled.
Approved: Cannot be edited, canceled, or sent for review again.
Rejected: Can be edited, resent for review, or canceled.
Canceled: Can no longer be modified.
